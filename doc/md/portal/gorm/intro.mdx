[GORM](https://gorm.io/index.html), a popular ORM in the Go community, simplifies database schema management with its AutoMigrate feature,
suitable for many development scenarios. For more advanced schema control, Atlas automates the process by comparing and
managing database states, integrating seamlessly with GORM projects.
